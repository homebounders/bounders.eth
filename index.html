The Team


Once upon a time, there was a group of people who called themselves the Home Bounders.

"Created by the poor for the poor."

Their mission was to bring hope and inspiration to those who suffer from a condition called "Filthy Rich." They knew what it felt like to have nothing and to be looked down upon by society. They understood the struggles of the less fortunate, and they wanted to make a difference.

One day, they discovered some little schemes that the wealthy had pulled against them. They felt outraged and decided to take action. With just a mop, a bucket, and some infested rats, they launched a plan to rival the wealthy. However, despite their best efforts, they lost the battle.

But their luck was about to change. One day, a group of Bounders who were working in the deep caves stumbled upon an emerald gem. They knew that the elite Covenant wanted it for themselves, so they started running. They didn't know where to go, but they had to get away fast.

Word of their discovery spread quickly, and soon, The Watchers were after them. The Bounders knew that they had to go deep underground to avoid being caught. Rumor has it that they're still hiding somewhere in the Underground of San Francisco, waiting for the perfect moment to make their move. The animals and creatures from the Underground heard about the Bounders' discovery and decided to join forces and help them. Together, they could bring down the wealthy elite and make the world a better place for everyone.



The Bounders have entered into an Inner Earth that would change their lives forever.
